What ReceiptFlow does

📸 One-tap capture

Open the app, point at a receipt, and tap. AI reads the merchant, date, amount, and category automatically.

☁️ Automatic cloud filing

Every receipt is renamed with a consistent format and filed into your Google Drive — organised into folders by status.

📊 Google Sheets log

A running spreadsheet is updated with each receipt so you always have a searchable, shareable expense record.

🔍 Review queue

Low-confidence scans go into a review queue so you can correct any details before they're filed permanently.
