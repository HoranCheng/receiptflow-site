ReceiptFlow

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 20, 2026

ReceiptFlow helps you capture receipts, extract structured receipt details, and store those receipts in your own Google Drive and Google Sheets.

Data We Process

ReceiptFlow may process:

  • Google account identity used for sign-in, such as your email address and Google user identifier.
  • Receipt photos or imported receipt images that you choose to scan.
  • Receipt details extracted from those images, such as merchant, date, total amount, currency, GST estimate, category, confidence score, file ID, and review status.
  • App settings and sync configuration, such as Google Drive folder IDs, Google Sheets spreadsheet ID, default currency, onboarding state, and sync timestamps.
  • Authentication tokens needed to keep you signed in and perform Google Drive and Sheets actions you request.

How Data Is Used

ReceiptFlow uses this data to:

  • Sign you in with Google.
  • Upload receipt images to folders in your Google Drive.
  • Create or update a Google Sheets receipt index.
  • Analyze receipt images and extract structured receipt information.
  • Restore your receipt metadata after reinstalling the app.
  • Display receipts, totals, categories, and review items inside the app.

ReceiptFlow does not sell your data and does not use your data for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Where Data Is Stored

ReceiptFlow stores data in these places:

  • On your device, using SwiftData, UserDefaults, local cache files, and Keychain.
  • In your Google Drive, inside folders created or found by the app.
  • In your Google Sheets, in a spreadsheet named receipt_index.
  • In a Cloudflare Worker request path used to analyze receipt images. Receipt images are sent to the worker for app functionality and are not used for advertising or tracking.

Google Drive and Google Sheets Access

ReceiptFlow requests Google permissions so it can:

  • Create and manage receipt files and folders in your Drive.
  • Read existing receipt files and Sheets metadata during restore.
  • Read and update the receipt index spreadsheet.

The app uses the drive.file scope, which limits access to only files and folders created by the app. It cannot read or modify any other files in your Drive.

You can revoke Google access from your Google Account settings at any time.

Photos and Camera

ReceiptFlow asks for camera access to photograph receipts and photo library access to import receipt images. When a receipt is captured with the camera, the app may save the cropped receipt image to your photo library as a local backup.

Data Deletion

You can sign out in the app to remove local authentication state. The app also includes a data deletion flow that clears local receipt data and can remove synced receipt data from Google Drive and Google Sheets.

You may also delete receipt files and spreadsheets directly from your Google Drive.

Tracking

ReceiptFlow does not use App Tracking Transparency tracking, advertising identifiers, third-party ad networks, or data brokers.

Children

ReceiptFlow is not directed to children and is intended for personal receipt organization.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, contact the developer through the support page or through the app listing in the App Store.
